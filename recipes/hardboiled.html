Hard boiled eggs

Description

Hard boiled eggs are probablt the most braindead but efficent way to make eggs. I dont like eating them anymore because I used to eat the alot and I find them kind og bland unless I eat them with salt.

  • get as many eggs as you want to eat
  • salt
  • pot
  • water
  • lid

Instructions

  1. get your eggs
  2. put them in the pot
  3. cover the eggs with water
  4. put on stove and set on high
  5. wait tell water boils and turn off stove and put a lid on the pot
  6. wait 10 mins
  7. take eggs out and eat with salt
