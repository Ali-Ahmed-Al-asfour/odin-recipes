Ali-Oatmeal

Description

Oatmeal is one of my favorite meals because it is easy to make and contains simple and easy carbs. It also can have many varieties of fruits added to it.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups oatmeal
  • honey
  • milk
  • cinnamon
  • Half an apple

Instructions

  1. First, get your oatmeal and pour two cups into a bowl.
  2. Then, get milk and pour until the milk is a little bit more than level with the oatmeal.
  3. Microwave these, and while waiting, get an apple and cut it up.
  4. Get milk, honey, cinnamon, and the apple slices and mix them into the oatmeal.
  5. You should be good to go!
